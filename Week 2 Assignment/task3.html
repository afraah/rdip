Palindrome & Anagram

  • PALINDROME: As you all know, a palindrome is a word that reads the same when read forward or backward. Eg: “MADAM”.
  • ANAGRAM: An anagram is a string of words/word such that if you rearrange the letters of the string in a specific order, you get another MEANINGFUL WORD. Example: Dirty Room(String of words), Dormitory(Second Input)
